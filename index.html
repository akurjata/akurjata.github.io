About

Hello! I am a multimedia journalist covering Northern British Columbia for CBC Radio and cbc.ca.

I am also the culture columnist for Thimbleberry Magazine, and in my spare time I run the r/princegeorge community subreddit.

I am based in the city of Prince George, on the traditional and unceded territory of the Lheidli T'enneh First Nation.

In case you're wondering, my last name is pronounced "ker • ya • ta".
